Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of Insurance do you accept?

    Blue Cross Blue Shield PPO, Blue Cross HMO and HME with a referral, United Healthcare, Cigna, Tricare, Aetna, Medicare, Champ VA, Mutual of Omaha, Celtic, HealthSmart. Patients can call us if they are wondering if Dr. Cettie is In-Network with their insurance company and we can check for them.

  • Will this go away on its own?

    The best bet is to call us if you have questions or concerns about what is going on with your foot and we can let you know if we recommend that you come in to be seen by the doctor. If there is a sign of infection we will always recommend that the patient be seen by the doctor so he can get that cleared up

  • Do I need a referral?

    The best way to determine that is to look on their insurance card. If it has a PCP’s (Primary Care Physician) name and info or their card said HMO then they will need a referral. If their insurance required that they select a PCP or had it chosen for them by the insurance company that they have to go to, they will also need a referral to be seen by Dr. Cettie. If they are still unsure, they should call the number on the back of their card and they can get that information from the insurance company.

  • Where are you located?

    The address is 2921 Southwest Pkwy. We are located at the corner of K-Mart Drive and Southwest Pkwy.

  • Can I be billed for the services?

    Dr. Cettie requires payment in full at the time of service. If the patient has insurance then we will collect the copay amount as listed on their insurance card.

  • I have a question about my bill, who should I talk to?

    We personally handle all billing in the office. Please call us at 940-692-7078 with any questions.

  • Do I get a discount if I don’t have insurance and am paying in cash?

    We personally handle all billing in the office. Please call us at 940-692-7078 with any questions.

  • What do I need to bring to my appointment?

    If you have never been seen before by Dr. Cettie we ask that you arrive 10-15 minutes before your appointment because we will have paperwork for you to fill out. You will need to bring a picture ID and your insurance card with you so we can scan it into our system. If you are taking any medications or have had previous hospitalizations or operations you will need to bring that information with you as well. If you have it in a typed or written list we can get a copy of it so you do not have to write it all out. Donuts or cookies are also always appreciated! : )

  • I need my toenails cut and cannot get pedicures or do it myself, does Dr. Cettie do this, and is it covered by my insurance?

    The trimming of the nails, corns, or calluses is considered routine foot care. This is not a covered service by many insurances including Medicare. Because of this Dr. Cettie charges a flat rate of $105 that is collected at the time of the visit. As a service to our patients we will bill this to your insurance and see if they will cover this service. If they do cover it in full or partially we reimburse the difference to the patient.
