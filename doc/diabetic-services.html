Our Diabetic Services

Dr. Comfort Shoes

Choose from a variety of shoes in modern and stylish styles. Medicare will pay for 1 pair of shoes per year, casting is quick and easy and done in the office, each pair of shoes comes with three custom inserts that are molded to your foot, shoes help with circulation and the prevent ulcerations from occurring under the weight bearing bones of your foot.

Diabetic Neuropathy Treatment

Neuropathy characteristics are feet burning at night like they are on hot coals, also early symptoms include numbness and tingling sensation in the feet, as it progressively worsens you may experience burning to your toes and bottoms and soles of feet especially at night, help treat through Neuremedy which can purchased in the office, it is a fat soluble B-12 vitamin that is much more effective than the water soluble B-12 purchased at pharmacies or health food stores; also recommend diabetic shoes

Diabetic Foot Care

In addition to customized footwear, we provide personalized footcare including trimming of nails, corns, and calluses, as well as routine work for the prevention and treatment of ulcers
