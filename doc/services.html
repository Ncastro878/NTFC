Services

Diagnostic Ultrasound Machine

The ultrasound machine is used to diagnose certain foot problems and to perform ultrasound guided injections that allow Dr. Cettie to precisely see that the injection is going in where it will be most effective.

Richie Braces

Treatment of ankle pain and chronic foot pain associated with instability, arthritis, and nerve damage. Custom fabrication done by Dr. Cettie based off of a foam cast impression that is done in the office

Routine Foot Care

Trimming of the corns, calluses, and toenails. Older patients who are unable to reach their feet and diabetic patients who are unable to trim their own nails most commonly require this. This needs to be done every 3-6 months for most patients.

In office surgery & X-rays

Dr. Cettie performs all surgeries in the office using local anesthesia. This saves the patient the cost and inconvenience of being admitted into the hospital for the operation. If the patient requires a surgery to correct a bunion Dr. Cettie refers the patient out to Dr. Brian Carpenter DPM in Decatur,TX.
